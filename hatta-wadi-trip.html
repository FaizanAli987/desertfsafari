Hatta Wadi Trip

We head for the magnificent Hajjar Mountains in luxury four wheel drive vehicles. This tour combines history, culture and adventure. Take a trip into the past with a visit to the Portuguese fort, which was built in the 14th century. We stop in Hatta for a visit to the wonderfully restored Heritage village where you can see a display of a typical village life. The village of Hatta, with the majestic mountain backdrop offers excellent photographic opportunities. We leave Hatta behind and continue driving through Wadis, (ancient dried up riverbeds).On our return we stop at the Hatta Forte Hotel for lunch. Then continue back to Dubai passing the street markets that sell Carpets and locally made pottery.

  • Departs daily @ 8:00 AM - 8:30 AM
  • Includes lunch & soft drinks or mineral water
  • Duration approx. 5 hrs
