Dune Buggy Safari

The dune buggy is one of the forms of off-road adventure in Dubai. This is an extremely popular activity among the residents and the thrill seeking tourists as well. Dune buggies are all terrain vehicles that can be self-driven on the desert dunes. The buggies are equipped with a safety harness and 4/5 point harness seat belt for the safety of the guests. Riders are also provided with a safety helmet and goggles. You can choose between 1 hour or 2 hours of dune buggy safari. This dune buggy safari is operated during the morning but, we recommend to couple this with our evening desert safari for extreme experience.

Our brand new Polaris dune buggies add to experience!

Note: Pick up timings may vary depending on sunrise timings.
