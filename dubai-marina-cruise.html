Dubai Marina Cruise

Splendid view of Dubai Marina while cruising on a floating restaurant on a traditional wooden dhow.

The second most popular attraction after the desert safari in Dubai is the dhow cruise. A ‘Dhow’ is a traditional wooden boat which was used by the Emiratis in the past for transport of both people and goods. These dhows have now been modernized and have become a major attraction among the tourists and locals here. The dhow has a fully air-conditioned lower deck and an open upper deck.

Explore the present-day Dubai in an old-fashioned way with photographic views, appetizing buffet dinner and live entertainment. On this 90 minutes cruise you will see the Marina Promenade with rows of yachts and boats, Dubai Marina Mall, Pier 7, the tallest twisted residential tower, Dubai Marina Yacht Club and more. The main highlight of this cruise is the tanoura dance show performed by a male artist. A variety of veg and non-veg delicacies are served at the buffet with soft drinks, tea, coffee and water.

Pick up and drop off facilities can also be arranged.

Inclusions & Exclusions:

  • Two way transfers from and to the hotel (centrally located hotel in Dubai)
  • 90 minutes cruising along the Dubai Marina
  • Welcome drinks on arrival
  • International buffet dinner including a variety of veg and non-veg options
  • Access to a fully air conditioned upper-deck and open air upper deck
  • Live entertainment - Tanoura dance show
  • Washroom facilities

When will I be picked up?

Pick up time starts around 1900 hours depending on hotel location.

When will I be back?

You should be back at the hotel by 2300 hours.

What are the ideal clothes for this safari?

We recommend casual clothing and don’t forget to carry your camera!
