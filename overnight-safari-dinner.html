OVERNIGHT DESERT SAFARI

Overnight Desert Safari is among the best Safari Tours in Dubai! It is an extension of the popular Evening Desert Safari; you can spend your night in open arabian desert camp and stay under the blanket of stars. Nothing can compare the thrill of sleeping underneath the beautiful desert sky. Overnight Desert Safari Dubai is a great way to experience the sights and sounds of Arabian Desert night in acompany with mesmerizing stars. The safari tour starts with an thrilling adventurous dune bashing drive that is followed by an array of fun activities. As the sun sets, unwind in our desert camp that replicates a Bedouin ambiance or recuperate by the fire until we serve you a delicious BBQ buffet dinner with a delectable range of salads and arabic desserts.

We’ll arrange you superb facilities for whole-night stay in our desert camp. In the morning, you can enjoy the stunning desert sunrise while enjoying your delicious breakfast, before you leave the wilderness of the Arabian Desert. If you are a nature lover and want to extend your arabian experience in Dubai, then book an overnight desert safari tour.
